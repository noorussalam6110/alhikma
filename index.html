Our Criteria

Comprehensive Evaluation Framework

Our comprehensive evaluation criteria ensure fair and constructive assessment of all artwork entries

1

Technical Skill

Assessment of artistic technique, craftsmanship, and proficiency with chosen medium and materials.

2

Creativity & Originality

Evaluation of originality, innovative approach, and unique creative expression in the artwork.

3

Composition & Design

Analysis of visual organization, balance, harmony, and effective use of design principles.

4

Thematic Relevance

How effectively the artwork addresses the competition theme and communicates intended concepts.

5

Emotional Impact

Ability to evoke appropriate emotional response, reflection, or intellectual engagement.

6

Educational Value

Assessment of learning demonstrated and appropriate content for educational setting.

Our Process

Systematic Evaluation Process

A systematic, transparent approach to ensure fair evaluation of all entries

1

Initial Submission Review

All entries are received and organized anonymously to ensure impartial evaluation from the beginning.

2

Criteria-Based Assessment

Each artwork is evaluated by multiple judges using our standardized criteria scoring system.

3

Panel Deliberation

Judges discuss all entries, compare evaluations, and reach consensus on rankings and awards.

4

Feedback Development

Constructive feedback is prepared for all participants to support artistic growth and development.

5

Results & Recognition

Final results are announced with clear explanations of selection criteria and artistic merit.
