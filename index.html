Evaluation Criteria

Comprehensive Assessment Framework

Our standardized criteria ensure fair and constructive evaluation of all artwork

1

Technical Skill

Assessment of artistic technique, craftsmanship, and medium proficiency with attention to detail.

2

Creativity & Originality

Evaluation of innovative approach, unique expression, and creative problem-solving.

3

Composition & Design

Analysis of visual organization, balance, harmony, and effective use of design principles.

4

Thematic Relevance

How effectively the artwork communicates intended concepts and addresses competition themes.

5

Emotional Impact

Ability to evoke appropriate emotional responses, reflection, or intellectual engagement.

6

Educational Value

Assessment of learning demonstrated and age-appropriate content for educational settings.

Our Process

Systematic Evaluation

Transparent approach ensuring fair evaluation of all entries

1

Submission Review

All entries are received and organized anonymously for impartial evaluation from the beginning.

2

Criteria Assessment

Each artwork is evaluated by multiple judges using our standardized criteria scoring system.

3

Panel Deliberation

Judges discuss all entries, compare evaluations, and reach consensus on rankings.

4

Feedback Development

Constructive feedback is prepared for all participants to support artistic growth.

5

Results Announcement

Final results are announced with clear explanations of selection criteria and artistic merit.
