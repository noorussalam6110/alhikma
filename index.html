Our Criteria

Comprehensive Evaluation Framework

Fair and constructive assessment of all artwork entries

1

Technical Skill

Assessment of artistic technique and craftsmanship with chosen medium.

2

Creativity & Originality

Evaluation of innovative approach and unique creative expression.

3

Composition & Design

Analysis of visual organization, balance, and design principles.

4

Thematic Relevance

How effectively artwork addresses the competition theme.

Emotional Impact

Educational Value
